Corporate Social Responsibility

At inLoop, we are always looking to practice CSR. That’s why we are donating 10% of all profits to Room to Read to promote children’s literacy and girls’ education across Asia and Africa. Each month, we will also be highlighting a new social issue in our weekly quizzes and on our social media platforms.

FAQ

How do I earn points? 

Simply read the new blurbs under News Highlights, and complete the weekly quizzes under Play.

How does the point system work? 

Each weekly quiz will test you on 15 news articles with 15 questions. Each correct answer is worth 5 points while each incorrect answer is worth 0 points.

How do prizes work? 

Prizes will be coming soon. There will be both weekly prizes and monthly prizes. See Prizes and Sponsors for more.

Will I get notified of my ranking each week? 

Only the top 10 players will be displayed on the leaderboard. If you would like to know your exact ranking, please email inloop.ja@gmail.com. 

How often are the Player Highlights updated? 

They are updated on a weekly basis. 

What are the quizzes going to test me one? 

The questions will all be knowledge-based. That means that as long as you read our news blurbs carefully, you’ll be well-prepared for the quizzes. 

How often should I play? 

Our news blurbs and quizzes are updated weekly so you should play once a week in order to climb up the leaderboard! See the countdown on Play to see the exact day of each update. 

How long will the quiz take me to complete? 

Completing the quiz will take approximately 10 minutes. 

I would like to work with inLoop; who should I contact? 

Please email inloop.ja@gmail.com.

What do I do if my question was not answered here? 

Please email inloop.ja@gmail.com.
